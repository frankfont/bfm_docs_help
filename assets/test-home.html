How it works

Twigflo missions are built with "twigs" that connect to your defined goal. Add cost, effort, and people to each twig and execute with intent. Create a story of your project effortlessly.

Define a goal

Every mission begins with a clear and achievable goal. Create a goal that all effort will anchor to.

Build

Drag, drop, and connect twigs within the mission canvas. Create a story that explains your project's resource management, budget, effort, and more.

Execute

Use your mission as a real-time status tracker for your projects. Update status, add and remove twigs, and showcase missions throughout the project lifecycle.
