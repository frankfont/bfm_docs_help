Effort Totals

Subscribers to the Pro package can enter and view effort estimates on individual workitems of a mission.

This provides a more granular insight into expected effort for a workitem beyond the inference conveyed by the selected workitem type shape.

The smart effort totals feature is activated by entering at least one effort value into a workitem.

Units of Effort

Entries are numeric and by default interpreted to be hours. Values can also include suffixes as explained below.

Comment Suffix Meaning Example
Hours can be entered as whole hours or fractions of hours. h Hours 5.5h
Days can be entered as whole days or fractions of days. The system assumes there are 8 hours in a day. d Days 2.25d
Weeks can be entered as whole weeks or fractions of weeks. The system assumes there are 40 hours in a week. w Weeks 1w

Effort Totals

Totals are computed and displayed throughout the mission diagram. A small symbol indicates whether a workitem contained a user provided effort entry or is simply the running total at that point in the workflow.
