Diagraming User Interface

Mission diagrams can be created and adjusted quickly through a variety of interface features.

Workitem Interaction

Create new unconnected Workitem Click the Create Workitem button at top of display.
Create new connected Workitem Hold the ALT key and RIGHT CLICK on an existing connected workitem.
Edit existing Workitem RIGHT CLICK on an existing workitem to get the Edit Workitem dialog.
Delete existing Workitem Click the Destroy button on the Edit Workitem dialog.

Fast Connection Steps

Connect workitems together Hold ALT key and drag green line.
Delete existing connection Hold SHIFT key and drag red line.
Replace connections with a new connection Hold SHIFT and ALT keys and drag purple line.

Alternate Connection Steps

Connect workitems together LEFT CLICK existing workitem and hold until a green symbol appears. Then drag green line.
Delete existing connection RIGHT CLICK on the line then press the DELETE key or the BACKSPACE key to remove it.

Other

Detangle Hold CTRL key and then RIGHT CLICK existing target workitem to detangle connections pointing toward that workitem. Alternatively, drag the root workitem of the tree.
Apply several times until you see results you want.
Save Layout Click the Save Layout button if you would like the relative positions of the workitems to be preserved for next time.
Release Editor Lock Click the Release Editor Lock button if you would like to let someone else edit the mission diagram.
Workitem Dimming Click the Focus Work drop-down control found at the top of the screen and select the workitem statuses that matter most to you. The others will be dimmed on the display.
Show and Hide Workitems Click the + symbol on the connector to show hidden workitems. Click the - symbol on a connector to hide workitems.
Zoom In and Zoom Out Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out. To restore the display back to default, click the Reset Display text when it appears at the top of the diagram.

General Tips

Workitems The mission diagram is composed of connected workitems. Different icons give a quick visual clue of the expected effort or complexity.
Effort Type
Highest Epic/Mission
High Story
Moderate Goal
Low Task
A Mission type is comparable to the Epic type and suggests you will model the linked details in another diagram.
Mission Diagram All your workitems lead to your mission goal.
  • The mission goal is found at the far right of the diagram.
  • Complete your workitems from left to right.
  • Reconfigure your diagram as mood and insight strike you so you are always working your way toward the final mission goal.
Focus Work In the upper right select the workitem statuses that you want to focus on. A good default is to focus on everything except completed and abandoned.
