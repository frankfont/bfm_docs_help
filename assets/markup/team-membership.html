Team Membership

Members of a team can interact with missions and templates that are team scope and in that team.

Team managers can choose who to add into a team and what rights they will have in that team.

Editing membership of a team

Click the team membership icon at the left of the main mission screen to see/edit membership of the currently selected team.

Adding members

Click the button and then select the user you would like added to the team.

Rights of team members

  • Can Read: Can view any content in the team but cannot change it.
  • Can Edit: Can view and change any content in the team but cannot delete missions.
  • Can Delete: Has all the rights of an editor but cannot add or remove members from the team.
  • Can Manage Team: Has all the rights of an editor and can add and remove members from the team.

Removing team members

Click the icon on the expanded member panel.
