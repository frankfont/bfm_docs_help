Team Membership

Members of a team can interact with missions and templates that are team scope and in that team.

Owners and administrators of teams can choose who to add into a team and what rights they will have in that team.

Adding members to a team

Click the group membership icon at the left of the main mission screen to see/edit membership of the currently selected team.

Rights of team members

  • Can Read: Can view any content in the team but cannot change it.
  • Can Edit: Can view and change any content in the team but cannot delete missions.
  • Can Delete: Has all the rights an editor but cannot add or remove members from the team.
  • Can Manage Team: This user has all the rights of an editor and can add and remove members from the team.

You can edit the membership at any time by clicking the same icon.
